Traditional Scottish Porridge

from thespruceeats.com

Total: 12 mins Servings: 2 Prep: 5 mins Cook: 7 mins

Ingredients

110 g Scottish pinhead oats or 110 g rolled oats

275 ml milk or 275 ml water or 275 ml milk and water mixture

(Optional) pinch salt

(Optional) to taste brown sugar or to taste honey

Steps

Gather the ingredients.

Place oats in saucepan with milk or water

Gently bring the oats to a slow boil, stirring all the time until the porridge begins to thicken.
